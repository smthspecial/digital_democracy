## Context

Concentrated, persistent governance work breeds political elites, burnout, and stable bribery networks.

## Decision

Distribute review and audit work through randomized assignment weighted by impact and competency, and make all oversight roles time-limited and continuously rotated.

## Consequences

Enormous distributed review/audit capacity, no permanent governing class, and disrupted capture networks. Cost: onboarding overhead and variable reviewer expertise. Realizes FR-050, FR-055, FR-062 and NFR-008.
