## Context

The platform itself can become a point of capture; technology should support governance, not define it, and 'blockchain fixes everything' is not a credible design stance.

## Decision

Make all governance-critical software and specifications open source and openly published, adopting cryptographic verification and append-only logging where they serve governance goals rather than as a default ideology.

## Consequences

The platform is publicly auditable and resistant to operator capture; technology choices stay subordinate to governance needs. Cost: requires disciplined evaluation of when distributed-ledger techniques are actually warranted. Realizes NFR-005, NFR-008, NFR-012, NFR-015.
