## Context

AI can help summarize preferences, detect conflicts, and generate alternatives, but granting it decision authority would undermine democratic legitimacy and transparency.

## Decision

Permit transparent AI only for analysis and synthesis (summaries, shared objectives, conflict detection, alternative generation, tradeoff estimates). AI holds no decision-making authority; all output is subject to human review and approval.

## Consequences

Reduced unnecessary conflict and faster proposal development without ceding sovereignty. Risk: over-reliance or hidden bias, mitigated by transparency and mandatory human review. Realizes FR-031.
