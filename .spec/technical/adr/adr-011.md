## Context

Rapid or unilateral execution of sensitive actions enables capture, coordinated manipulation, and emotional policy swings.

## Decision

Require high-impact and protocol-level actions to pass multiple independent approvals and mandatory delay periods (public review window, challenge period, verification interval) before execution.

## Consequences

Capture and manipulation become observable and contestable before taking effect. Cost: slower response for genuinely urgent matters. Realizes FR-061, FR-065, FR-067 and FR-044, NFR-008.
