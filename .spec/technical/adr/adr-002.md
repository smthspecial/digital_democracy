## Context

The system must simultaneously verify voter eligibility (one person, one vote) and preserve ballot secrecy, while letting voters confirm their vote counted and resisting coercion and vote-buying.

## Decision

Store eligibility credentials and cast ballots in cryptographically separated domains, using verifiable-but-anonymous voting techniques. Provide individual verifiability without receipts that prove a vote to third parties.

## Consequences

Secret ballot and verifiability coexist; operators cannot deanonymize voters. Cost: significant cryptographic complexity and key-management requirements. Realizes FR-003, FR-004 and NFR-001, NFR-003.
