## Context

Continuous direct participation across all domains is unsustainable, but permanent delegation recreates a representative class.

## Decision

Allow citizens to delegate voting within specific domains to trusted specialists, with delegation voluntary, transparent, issue-specific, revocable at any time, and automatically expiring.

## Consequences

Reduced fatigue while preserving citizen sovereignty and preventing permanent delegates. Cost: delegation-graph management and abuse monitoring. Realizes FR-056, FR-057.
