## Context

Corruption resistance requires that governance actions cannot be silently altered or deleted, while voting privacy is still preserved.

## Decision

Record all governance-relevant actions in an append-only, cryptographically secured, publicly verifiable log; keep ballot content private but make system actions transparent.

## Consequences

Corruption can be exposed but not erased; integrity is independently verifiable. Cost: storage growth and careful separation of public actions from private votes. Realizes FR-060 and NFR-005, NFR-015.
