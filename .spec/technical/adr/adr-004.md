## Context

Requiring every citizen to vote on every issue causes fatigue, uninformed participation, and political noise; decision authority should align with who is affected.

## Decision

Assign each proposal one of six impact-scope levels and derive voting eligibility from it, granting authority to the smallest competent jurisdiction and escalating only when scope is exceeded.

## Consequences

Reduced voting volume, higher relevance, stronger local ownership. Risks: jurisdiction disputes and gerrymandering, mitigated by public, fixed, independently reviewed boundaries. Realizes FR-008..FR-014 and NFR-014.
