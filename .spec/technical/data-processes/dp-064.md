## Description

Governs the election of protocol council members (AUTH-008). Protocol council is the only governance role obtained through a direct citizen election rather than random assignment.

### Election trigger

DP-050 (governance role rotation cron) detects that one or more `governance_role` rows with `role_type=protocol_council` are within 60 days of `term_end`, or a vacancy exists due to early departure. governance-role-service enqueues an election cycle to governance.protocol.

### Candidacy phase

1. Any active citizen (AUTH-001) may declare candidacy via `POST /elections/{id}/candidates`.
2. Candidate declaration requires full COI disclosure (DP-010) covering all domains and organizational affiliations.
3. Undisclosed conflicts discovered after declaration are grounds for disqualification (DP-033).
4. Candidacy declarations are public and included in the audit log (DP-036).
5. Candidacy window: 30 days.

### Election vote session

1. After candidacy closes, an election vote session is created (SRV-008) with `session_type=election`.
2. Eligible voters: all active citizens in the relevant jurisdiction.
3. Voting uses the standard cryptographic ballot pipeline (DP-025, DP-016, ADR-002) — ballot secrecy preserved even in elections.
4. Voting window: 14 days.

### Result certification

1. DP-026 tallies votes using the multi-option method (ADR-006).
2. DP-027 certifies the result. Certification requires:
   - Quorum met (minimum participation threshold).
   - Result confirmed by an independent audit body (AUTH-003).
3. Certified results are public and immutable in the audit log.

### Role activation

1. On certification: governance-role-service writes `governance_role` rows for elected members (TBL-032, role_type=protocol_council, term_start=election_certified_at, term_end=term_start + defined_term).
2. `randomized=false` (election, not random selection).
3. Outgoing council members' roles expire at their `term_end`; no gap in council coverage because elections begin before terms expire.
4. Immediate COI check (DP-033): any newly detected conflict causes role suspension pending resolution.
5. All activations emitted to audit.append (DP-036).

### Term limits

DP-050 enforces `term_end`. A protocol council member may not serve consecutive terms without a mandatory cooling-off period of one full term length (ADR-009).
