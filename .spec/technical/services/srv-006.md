## Responsibility

Hosts structured public deliberation on proposals: threaded evidence-linked arguments and citizen preference declarations. Enforces that arguments must reference evidence and that agreement/disagreement stances are structurally separated. Feeds preference data to the AI synthesis service for advisory summaries.

## Owned Tables

| Table | Role |
|-------|------|
| TBL-017 deliberation_argument | Primary owner — threaded evidence-linked arguments |
| TBL-018 preference | Primary owner — desired-outcome declarations |

## Operations

| Operation | Mode | Process |
|-----------|------|---------|
| Post deliberation argument | sync | DP-008 |
| Declare preference | sync | DP-009 |
| AI policy synthesis | async | DP-037 (triggered when argument/preference volume crosses threshold) |

## Key rules

- Every `deliberation_argument` must have a non-null `evidence_ref`. Submissions without evidence are rejected at write time (FR-028).
- `stance` enum (`agreement` | `disagreement`) is required; it separates areas of consensus from contested methods (FR-033, ADR-012).
- `parent_id` enables threaded replies. The review body may lock a factual `agreement` branch to prevent re-litigation of established facts (FR-033).
- `preference` rows are captured before specific solutions are proposed (FR-030); the `problem_id` reference ensures preferences are tied to the problem, not a particular proposal.
- DP-037 fires asynchronously when the count of new arguments or preferences on a proposal exceeds a configurable threshold. The AI worker reads arguments and preferences (advisory only, ADR-007) and writes its synthesis output to a separate advisory store; it does not write back to these tables.
- No argument or preference can restrict a citizen's right to vote (FR-049).

## Dependencies

- Emits to: `ai-synthesis-service` (DP-037), `audit-service` (DP-036 on argument post)
- Read by: `proposal-service` (constraint alignment), `voting-service` (voting package assembly, FR-043), `reputation-service` (constructive contribution signals)
