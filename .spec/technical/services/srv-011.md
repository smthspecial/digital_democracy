## Responsibility

Manages time-limited governance roles (auditor, reviewer, oversight, operator, review_body), multi-approval coordination for critical actions, and role rotation. Enforces that no role can be held permanently and that powerful actions require independent approvals from different role types (ADR-001, ADR-011).

## Key rules

- `governance_role.randomized=true` marks roles selected at random (FR-062). Citizens cannot self-nominate for randomized roles.
- `term_end` is mandatory. DP-050 runs daily and identifies roles whose `term_end < now() + 7 days`, triggers off-boarding notifications, and enqueues DP-040 to generate replacement assignments.
- Multi-approval (DP-035) tracks required approval types: `citizen_supermajority`, `audit_confirmation`, `body_endorsement`. All three must be satisfied before a critical action can proceed. Each `approval` row has `approver_role_id` linking to an independent `governance_role`. A single person cannot supply multiple approval types for the same action.
- Protocol changes (FR-067) additionally require: supermajority citizen vote, independent audit confirmation, time-delay enforcement (DP-043), and public visibility of the pending change during the delay window.
- A citizen with a COI in the affected domain (from DP-033) is automatically excluded from the approval pool for that action.
- All role assignments, term expirations, and approval decisions are emitted to `audit-service`.
