## Description

As a voter, I can evaluate multiple options using ranked or approval methods so that complex issues are not forced into yes/no.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Proposals can present more than two options
- [ ] At least one non-binary voting method is supported
- [ ] Results identify broadly acceptable solutions

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-032
- Technical aspects: ADR-006
- Multi-option decision framework.
