## Description

As a system, I can automatically exclude conflicted participants from decisions so that self-interest cannot quietly steer governance infrastructure.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Infrastructure participants must declare conflicts
- [ ] Conflicted participants are auto-excluded from affected decisions
- [ ] Declarations are auditable
