## Description

As a citizen, I can delegate my vote in a specific domain to a trusted specialist so that I can stay represented in domains I cannot follow closely.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Delegation is opt-in and per-domain
- [ ] Delegations are transparent and recorded
- [ ] Delegation does not transfer across unrelated domains

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-056
- Technical aspects: ADR-008
- Liquid democracy, issue-specific.
