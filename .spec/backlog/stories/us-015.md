## Description

As a domain expert, I can publish formal analysis without gaining voting power so that expertise informs decisions without ruling them.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] I can publish evaluations, risk warnings, and impact assessments
- [ ] My analysis gains prominence but not voting weight
- [ ] I cannot veto, override votes, or block voting

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-020, FR-023
- Technical aspects: ADR-007, NFR-008
- Expertise earns the right to explain, not to rule.
