## Description

As a voter, I can see complete funding information on every proposal so that I vote on policy and spending together.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] No proposal reaches voting without cost, funding source, maintenance cost, and benefits
- [ ] Funding information is shown on the ballot
- [ ] Unfunded proposals cannot advance

- Implements requirements: FR-037
- Technical aspects: NFR-005
