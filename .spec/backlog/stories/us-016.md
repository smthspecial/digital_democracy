## Description

As a citizen, I can challenge a competency claim with evidence so that false or conflicted expertise can be removed.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] I can challenge credentials, conflicts, false claims, or misconduct
- [ ] Challenges require evidence; anonymous accusations are rejected
- [ ] Challenges follow a public review and decision process

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-024
- Technical aspects: NFR-004
- Prevents expert capture.
