## Description

As a voter, I can cast a secret ballot and later confirm it was counted so that I can trust the result without anyone learning how I voted.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] My ballot is cryptographically separated from my identity
- [ ] I can verify my vote is included in the tally without revealing its content
- [ ] I cannot produce a receipt that proves my vote to a third party

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-003, FR-004
- Technical aspects: ADR-002, NFR-001, NFR-003
- Coercion- and vote-buying-resistant.
