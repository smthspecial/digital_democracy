## Description

As a facilitator, I can use transparent AI to synthesize options from public input so that conflict is reduced without ceding any authority to AI.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] AI can summarize preferences, detect conflicts, and generate alternatives
- [ ] AI assistance is clearly labeled and has no decision authority
- [ ] All AI output is subject to human review and approval

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-031
- Technical aspects: ADR-007
- AI is advisory only.
