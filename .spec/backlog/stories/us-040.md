## Description

As a citizen, I can track my civic-duty quota and participation score so that I know what is expected and how I am contributing.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A small annual civic-duty quota is defined for me (target 2-4 hours/month)
- [ ] My participation score reflects votes, reviews, audits, and contributions
- [ ] The score never alters my voting weight

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-051, FR-052
- Technical aspects: NFR-014
- Civic duty credits.
