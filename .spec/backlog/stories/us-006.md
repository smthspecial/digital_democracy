## Description

As a citizen, I can see whether a proposal affects me and why so that I understand my eligibility before a vote.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Each proposal shows its assigned impact scope and rationale
- [ ] Eligibility criteria are explained in plain language
- [ ] A proposal cannot reach voting without an assigned scope

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-008, FR-009
- Technical aspects: ADR-004, NFR-010
- Transparency of eligibility.
