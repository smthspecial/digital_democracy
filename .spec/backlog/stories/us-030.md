## Description

As a voter, I can see long-term and future costs before voting so that short-term thinking is countered.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Major decisions include long-term impact and future-cost analysis
- [ ] Future and recurring costs are itemized before the vote

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-040
- Technical aspects: NFR-005
- Mitigates short-term thinking.
