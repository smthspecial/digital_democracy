## Description

As a auditor, I can compare measured outcomes against promised outcomes so that future decisions are informed by real results.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Measured outcomes are recorded after completion
- [ ] Results are compared to promised objectives
- [ ] Evaluations are public
