## Description

As a voter, I can vote only on proposals that affect me so that I am not overwhelmed by irrelevant decisions.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Eligibility is computed from the proposal's impact scope
- [ ] Non-affected citizens are excluded
- [ ] Eligibility decisions are explainable

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-041
- Technical aspects: ADR-004, NFR-014
- Impact-derived eligibility.
