## Description

As a resident, I can have my residency verified across the jurisdictions I belong to so that I can participate at every level that affects me.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] My residency is verified against a proposal's jurisdiction
- [ ] Configurable minimum residency periods are enforced
- [ ] I can hold simultaneous memberships across nested jurisdictions

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-012, FR-013
- Technical aspects: ADR-004, NFR-002
- Anti residency-manipulation.
