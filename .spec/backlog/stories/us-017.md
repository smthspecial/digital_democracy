## Description

As a domain contributor, I can disclose conflicts of interest relevant to my domain so that citizens can weigh my analysis fairly.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] I must disclose employers, ownership, consulting, and financial interests
- [ ] Disclosures are public alongside my analysis
- [ ] Undisclosed conflicts are grounds for challenge

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-025
- Technical aspects: ADR-009, NFR-005
- Conflict-of-interest registry.
