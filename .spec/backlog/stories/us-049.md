## Description

As a system, I can rotate and randomize oversight roles so that persistent capture networks are broken.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Auditors are randomly selected
- [ ] Oversight roles are time-limited
- [ ] Governance assignments rotate continuously

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-062
- Technical aspects: ADR-009
- Rotation defeats long-term capture.
