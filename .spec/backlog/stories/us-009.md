## Description

As a scope reviewer, I can assign one of six impact-scope levels to a proposal so that authority goes to the smallest competent jurisdiction.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] I can assign a property, street, municipality, regional, national, or constitutional level
- [ ] Authority defaults to the smallest competent jurisdiction
- [ ] Escalation to a higher level records a documented reason

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-014
- Technical aspects: ADR-004
- Implements the impact calculation framework.
