## Description

As a citizen, I can claim exemptions and have inactivity handled humanely so that life circumstances do not unfairly penalize me.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] I can claim verified exemptions that pause obligations without penalty
- [ ] Inactivity escalates through reminder, reduced assignments, then temporary inactive status
- [ ] I always retain constitutional protections and access to information

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-053, FR-054
- Technical aspects: NFR-013
- Absence and inactivity management.
