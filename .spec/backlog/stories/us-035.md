## Description

As a executing institution, I can carry out approved decisions without changing policy so that decisions are implemented efficiently while citizens keep authority.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Citizens decide what; institutions decide how
- [ ] Engineering, legal, and administrative details are not put to referendum
- [ ] Operators cannot alter the approved decision

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-045
- Technical aspects: ADR-001
- Decision/execution separation.
