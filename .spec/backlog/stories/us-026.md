## Description

As a proposer, I can define objectives and measurable outcomes for my proposal so that success can be judged on results, not rhetoric.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Each approved proposal declares objectives and measurable outcomes
- [ ] Expected costs and timeline are recorded
- [ ] Outcomes are later compared to promises

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-035
- Technical aspects: NFR-005
- Outcome-based governance.
