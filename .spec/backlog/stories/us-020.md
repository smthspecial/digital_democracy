## Description

As a citizen, I can take part in structured, evidence-linked deliberation so that discussion improves proposals instead of becoming a popularity contest.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Arguments must reference evidence, studies, budgets, or impact reports
- [ ] Discussion separates agreed facts from contested methods
- [ ] Established facts are not repeatedly re-litigated

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-028, FR-033
- Technical aspects: ADR-012, NFR-010
- Structured disagreement.
