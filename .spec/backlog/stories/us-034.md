## Description

As a system, I can enforce review and cooling-off periods on high-impact votes so that reactive, emotional decisions are avoided.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] High-impact decisions require a mandatory review period
- [ ] A cooling-off interval applies before the vote
- [ ] Period lengths scale with decision impact

## Notes

- Implements requirements: FR-044
- Technical aspects: ADR-011
- Mitigates emotional decision-making.
