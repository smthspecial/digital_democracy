## Description

Reduce participation fatigue by letting citizens temporarily delegate voting within specific domains to trusted specialists. Delegation is voluntary, transparent, revocable at any time, issue-specific, and never permanent.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Citizens can delegate specific voting domains to trusted specialists
- [ ] Delegation is voluntary, transparent, and revocable at any time
- [ ] Delegation is issue-specific and does not transfer across domains
- [ ] Delegations expire automatically; no permanent delegates exist

Source: White Paper System Design Layer 12 (Delegated Expertise), Module 4 Delegation Option. Implements FR-056 through FR-057.
