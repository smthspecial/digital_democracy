## Description

Establish a trusted civic-identity layer that guarantees one citizen = one account = one vote, verifies eligibility, preserves ballot secrecy, and resists fraud, surveillance, and platform capture. Without trusted identity every other governance mechanism is vulnerable.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Every citizen has exactly one active, verifiable civic identity
- [ ] Voting records are cryptographically separated from personal identity
- [ ] Voters can verify their vote was counted without revealing its content
- [ ] Identity creation, revocation, and administration are auditable and require multi-party approval
