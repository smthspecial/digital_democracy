## Description

Ensure decisions are made by the people affected by them. Each proposal is assigned an impact scope (property, street, municipality, region, national, constitutional) that determines eligible participants, reducing voter fatigue and aligning decision rights with consequences.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Every proposal carries a publicly visible, challengeable impact scope before voting can begin
- [ ] Eligibility is derived from verified residency and jurisdiction membership
- [ ] The smallest competent jurisdiction receives authority; issues escalate only when they exceed local scope
- [ ] Jurisdiction boundaries are public, fixed, and independently reviewed
