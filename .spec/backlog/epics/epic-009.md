## Description

Keep participation informed, fairly distributed, and sustainable. Provide neutral educational material before technical votes, distribute civic duties via randomized assignment, track participation with low workload, and manage absence and inactivity humanely.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Neutral educational materials and impact models precede votes on technical issues
- [ ] Civic duties (reviews, audits) are distributed through randomized assignment weighted by impact and competency
- [ ] Annual civic-duty workload remains intentionally low (target 2-4 hours/month)
- [ ] Absence is exemptible without penalty; inactivity is managed in escalating stages while preserving constitutional protections

Source: White Paper System Design Layer 11 (Civic Knowledge), Module 4 (Randomized Civic Assignment, Participation Scoring, Absence/Inactivity Management, Audit System), Principle 4, Civic Duty Credits. Implements FR-049 through FR-055.
