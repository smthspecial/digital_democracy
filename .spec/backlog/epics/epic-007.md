## Description

Convert public preferences into binding decisions with eligibility drawn from the sphere of impact and thresholds matched to decision impact. Supports multiple voting methods and protective review/cooling-off periods for high-impact decisions.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Voting eligibility is determined by sphere of impact
- [ ] Thresholds scale with decision type (local simple majority, national majority plus minimum participation, constitutional supermajority)
- [ ] Every voter receives a complete voting package (problem, solution, budget, expert analysis, alternatives)
- [ ] High-impact decisions require review and cooling-off periods before voting

Source: White Paper System Design Layer 8 (Voting Layer), Module 4 Voting Package, Risks (emotional decision-making). Implements FR-041 through FR-044.
