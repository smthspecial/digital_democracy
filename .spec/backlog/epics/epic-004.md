## Description

Improve decision quality by making domain expertise visible and auditable without granting experts additional sovereignty. Competency is domain-specific, earned, challengeable, time-limited, and confers the right to explain — never the right to rule.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All citizens remain politically equal regardless of competency
- [ ] Domain-specific competency levels are earned through an auditable acquisition process
- [ ] Competency grants visibility and review authority, never voting power or veto
- [ ] Competency claims, conflicts of interest, and reputation are public, challengeable, and expire periodically
