## Description

Connect every proposal to financial reality and give citizens direct, transparent influence over how public money is allocated. Public budgets are visible in real time, partitioned into categories, and shaped by participatory allocation.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A real-time public ledger shows revenue sources, allocations, and expenditures
- [ ] No proposal reaches voting without cost, funding source, maintenance cost, and expected benefits
- [ ] Citizens allocate priorities within partitioned budget categories
- [ ] Long-term and future costs are visible before votes occur
