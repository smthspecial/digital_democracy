## Description

The system shall provide neutral educational materials, simulations, impact models, and expert explanations before votes on highly technical issues, aiming for informed participation rather than gatekeeping.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Neutral educational material is available for technical proposals
- [ ] Multiple expert perspectives and impact assessments are provided
- [ ] Access to information is never used to restrict the right to vote
