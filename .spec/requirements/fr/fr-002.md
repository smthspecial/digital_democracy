## Description

The system shall link every civic identity to a verified legal identity, using methods such as national identity systems, passport verification, or government-issued credentials.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Identity verification is required before a civic identity becomes active
- [ ] At least one accepted verification method must succeed
- [ ] Verification evidence is retained for audit without becoming publicly visible
