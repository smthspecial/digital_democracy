## Description

The system shall let citizens evaluate multiple alternatives using methods such as ranked-choice, approval voting, preference scoring, or comparative ranking, rather than forcing binary choices.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Proposals can present more than two options
- [ ] At least one non-binary voting method is supported
- [ ] Results identify broadly acceptable solutions
