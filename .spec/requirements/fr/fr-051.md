## Description

The system shall require each citizen to complete a minimum, intentionally small level of civic participation (target 2-4 hours per month).

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A minimum annual civic-duty quota is defined per citizen
- [ ] Typical workload remains around 2-4 hours per month
- [ ] Quotas are derived from location and competencies
