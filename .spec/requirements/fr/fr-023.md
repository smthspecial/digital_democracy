## Description

The system shall grant competency holders enhanced visibility and the authority to publish evaluations, risk warnings, and impact assessments, while prohibiting them from vetoing proposals, overriding votes, creating binding decisions, or suppressing opposing opinions.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Experts can publish formal evaluations and warnings
- [ ] Expert analysis receives greater prominence but not greater voting power
- [ ] Experts cannot veto, override votes, or block voting
- [ ] Expert warnings never prevent a vote from proceeding
