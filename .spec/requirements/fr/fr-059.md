## Description

The system shall require constitutional changes to undergo national review, expert assessment, extended deliberation, and supermajority approval with elevated participation thresholds.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Constitutional changes require a supermajority
- [ ] Elevated participation thresholds apply
- [ ] Extended deliberation and expert assessment are mandatory
