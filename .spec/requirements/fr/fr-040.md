## Description

The system shall make long-term and future costs of major decisions visible before votes occur.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Major decisions include long-term impact and future-cost analysis
- [ ] Future costs are presented to voters before voting
- [ ] Maintenance and recurring costs are itemized
