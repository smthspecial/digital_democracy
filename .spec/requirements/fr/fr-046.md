The system shall give every approved project a timeline, budget tracking, milestone reporting, and outcome metrics.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Each approved project has a published timeline
- [ ] Budget consumption is tracked against the approved budget
- [ ] Milestones and outcome metrics are reported
