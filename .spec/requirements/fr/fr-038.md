## Description

The system shall allow citizens to express collective preferences over budget allocation across categories such as infrastructure, education, healthcare, defense, research, and environment.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Citizens can allocate preferences across budget categories
- [ ] Aggregated allocations inform the resulting budget
- [ ] Allocation results are public
