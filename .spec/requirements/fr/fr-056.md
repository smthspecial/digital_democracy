## Description

The system shall allow citizens to temporarily delegate voting within specific domains (e.g. energy, healthcare, transportation, education) to trusted specialists, voluntarily and transparently.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Delegation is opt-in and per-domain
- [ ] Delegations are transparent and recorded
- [ ] Delegation does not transfer across unrelated domains
