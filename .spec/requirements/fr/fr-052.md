## Description

The system shall maintain a participation score reflecting voting, reviews, audits, and proposal contributions, and recording repeated non-response or abandoned assignments.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Constructive participation increases the score
- [ ] Repeated non-response and abandoned assignments are recorded
- [ ] Scores never alter a citizen's voting weight
