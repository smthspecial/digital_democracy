## Description

The system shall require governance-infrastructure participants to declare financial interests, organizational affiliations, and indirect benefit exposure, and shall automatically exclude them from decisions where conflicts exist.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Infrastructure participants must declare conflicts
- [ ] Conflicted participants are automatically excluded from affected decisions
- [ ] Declarations are auditable
