## Description

The system shall compare measured outcomes of completed projects against their promised outcomes and publish the evaluation.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Measured outcomes are recorded after completion
- [ ] Results are compared against the promised objectives
- [ ] Evaluations are public and inform future decisions
