## Description

The system shall prevent any proposal from entering voting without displaying cost, funding source, maintenance cost, and expected benefits.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A proposal cannot reach voting without complete funding information
- [ ] Cost, funding source, maintenance cost, and expected benefits are shown
- [ ] Citizens vote on both policy and spending together
