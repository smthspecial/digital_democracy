## Description

The system shall apply review, public-debate, and cooling-off periods before voting on important or high-impact decisions to counter emotional, reactive decision-making.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] High-impact decisions require a mandatory review period before voting
- [ ] A cooling-off interval applies after deliberation and before the vote
- [ ] Period lengths scale with decision impact
