## Description

The system shall require every proposal to carry a defined impact scope; voting cannot begin without scope assignment.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A proposal cannot enter voting without an assigned impact scope
- [ ] Scope assignment is recorded before the voting phase opens
