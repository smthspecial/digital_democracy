## Description

The system shall structure discussions to distinguish areas of agreement (facts and objectives) from areas of disagreement (competing methods), preventing repeated debate of established facts.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Discussions separate agreed facts/objectives from contested methods
- [ ] Established facts are not re-litigated within a proposal
- [ ] Points of disagreement are explicitly catalogued
