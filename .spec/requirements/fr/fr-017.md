## Description

The system shall require a proposal to demonstrate sufficient support before it becomes a vote, with thresholds that scale with population (e.g. neighborhood 50, city 500, national 50,000).

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A proposal advances only after reaching its support threshold
- [ ] Thresholds scale with the population of the impact scope
- [ ] Threshold values are public and configurable per jurisdiction
