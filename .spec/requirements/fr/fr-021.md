## Description

The system shall support domain-specific competency levels (Level 0 General Citizen through Level 4 Senior Domain Contributor) that do not transfer between domains.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Competency is recorded per domain and level
- [ ] Competency in one domain does not grant status in another
- [ ] Domains operate independently
