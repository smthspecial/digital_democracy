## Description

The system shall separate decision rights from execution rights: citizens decide what should happen while professional institutions decide how to implement approved decisions.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Citizens hold authority over what is decided
- [ ] Institutions hold authority over implementation method and logistics
- [ ] Engineering, legal, and administrative details are not put to public referendum
