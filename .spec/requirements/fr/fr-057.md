## Description

The system shall make delegations revocable at any time and automatically expiring, so no permanent delegates emerge.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A citizen can revoke a delegation at any time
- [ ] Delegations expire automatically after a defined period
- [ ] No delegate can hold delegated authority permanently

## Traceability

- Source: White Paper Layer 12; Anti-Capture (no permanent delegates).
