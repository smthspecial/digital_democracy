## Description

The system shall hold fundamental rights (freedom of speech, due process, equality before law, property rights, human rights) outside the reach of ordinary voting.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A defined set of protected rights cannot be altered by ordinary votes
- [ ] Proposals that violate protected rights are flagged in constitutional review
- [ ] Constitutional review can block rights-violating proposals
