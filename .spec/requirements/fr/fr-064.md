## Description

The system shall operate all governance roles under fixed, transparent compensation rules with no discretionary bonuses, hidden rewards, or private incentives.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Compensation rules are fixed and transparent
- [ ] No discretionary bonuses or hidden rewards exist
- [ ] Compensation cannot be used as a corruption lever
