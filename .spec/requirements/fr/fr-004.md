## Description

The system shall allow citizens to verify that their vote was counted without revealing its content.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A voter can confirm inclusion of their ballot in the final tally
- [ ] Verification does not disclose the vote choice to anyone
- [ ] Verification artifacts cannot be used to prove a vote to a third party (coercion resistance)
