## Description

The system shall require every approved proposal to define objectives, measurable outcomes, expected costs, and an implementation timeline, and shall compare measured outcomes against expectations after implementation.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Each approved proposal declares objectives and measurable outcomes
- [ ] Expected costs and timeline are recorded
- [ ] Post-implementation results are compared to promises and published
