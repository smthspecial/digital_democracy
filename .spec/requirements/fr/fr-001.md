## Description

The system shall ensure every citizen possesses exactly one active civic identity, so that one citizen equals one account and one vote.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A citizen cannot hold more than one active civic identity at a time
- [ ] Duplicate-identity creation attempts are detected and rejected
- [ ] Each civic identity maps to exactly one verified legal person
