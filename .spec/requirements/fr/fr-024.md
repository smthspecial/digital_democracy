## Description

The system shall keep every competency claim continuously reviewable, allowing any citizen or peer to challenge credentials, conflicts of interest, false claims, or misconduct, subject to an evidence requirement.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Any citizen can challenge a competency claim with evidence
- [ ] Domain peers can initiate competency reviews
- [ ] Anonymous, evidence-free accusations are rejected
- [ ] Challenges follow a public review and decision process
