## Description

The system shall provide structured deliberation, distinct from social media, in which arguments are linked to evidence, studies, budgets, and impact reports.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Deliberation spaces are structured rather than popularity-ranked
- [ ] Arguments must reference evidence, studies, budgets, or impact reports
- [ ] The goal is to improve proposal quality before voting
