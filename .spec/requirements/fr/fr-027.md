## Description

The system shall maintain a reputation signal, separate from competency, reflecting accurate predictions, constructive participation, transparent disclosures, and successful proposals, and penalizing misinformation, undisclosed conflicts, manipulation, and fraud.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Reputation is tracked independently of competency level
- [ ] Positive and negative reputation factors are defined and transparent
- [ ] Reputation never alters voting weight
