## Description

The system shall require domain contributors to disclose employers, ownership interests, consulting relationships, and financial interests relevant to their domain.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Domain contributors must disclose relevant conflicts before participating
- [ ] Disclosures are publicly visible alongside expert analysis
- [ ] Undisclosed conflicts are grounds for challenge
