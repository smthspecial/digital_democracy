## Description

The system may use transparent AI to summarize preferences, identify shared objectives, detect conflicts, generate alternatives, and estimate tradeoffs, with no decision-making authority and all output subject to human review.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] AI assistance is clearly labeled and transparent
- [ ] AI has no decision-making authority
- [ ] All AI-generated proposals remain subject to human review and approval
