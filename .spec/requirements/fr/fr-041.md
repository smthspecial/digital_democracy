## Description

The system shall determine voting eligibility for each proposal from its sphere of impact, so only affected citizens vote.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Eligibility is computed from the proposal's impact scope
- [ ] Non-affected citizens are excluded from the vote
- [ ] Eligibility decisions are explainable to citizens
