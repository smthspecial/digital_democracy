## Description

The system shall make impact-scope assignment publicly visible so citizens understand why they are or are not eligible.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] The assigned scope and its rationale are publicly displayed on each proposal
- [ ] Eligibility criteria for the proposal are explained to citizens
