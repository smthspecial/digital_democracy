## Description

The system shall operate multiple independent audit bodies simultaneously, supporting competing interpretations, cross-validation of findings, and public disagreement resolution to avoid single-point oversight failure.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] More than one independent audit body operates concurrently
- [ ] Audit findings are cross-validated
- [ ] Disagreements between auditors are resolved publicly
