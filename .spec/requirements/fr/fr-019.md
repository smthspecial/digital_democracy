## Description

The system shall provide a development phase, after a proposal reaches threshold, in which participants submit alternatives, modifications, and cost estimates.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A proposal that reaches threshold enters a development phase
- [ ] Participants can submit alternatives, modifications, and cost estimates
- [ ] Competing solutions may coexist through development
