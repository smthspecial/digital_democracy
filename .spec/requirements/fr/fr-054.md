## Description

The system shall manage persistent inactivity through escalating stages (reminder, reduced assignments, temporary inactive status) while preserving constitutional protections and access to information.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Inactivity escalates through defined stages
- [ ] Inactive citizens retain constitutional protections and information access
- [ ] Only advanced governance privileges are paused until participation resumes
