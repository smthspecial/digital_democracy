## Description

The system shall randomly select citizens into audit pools for budget review, project verification, and outcome evaluation, supported by relevant domain contributors, with findings made public.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Citizens are randomly selected into audit assignments
- [ ] Domain experts support audits without controlling them
- [ ] Audit findings are published

## Traceability

- Source: White Paper Module 4 Audit System; Anti-Capture (no permanent auditors).
