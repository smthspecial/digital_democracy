## Description

The system shall provide a staged deadlock resolution framework (constraint analysis, alternative generation, resource partitioning, compensation assessment, citizen assembly review, escalation review, constitutional review, final decision procedure) so no process remains indefinitely blocked.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] All eight resolution stages are defined and ordered
- [ ] A blocked proposal advances through the stages until resolved
- [ ] No governance process can remain permanently blocked
