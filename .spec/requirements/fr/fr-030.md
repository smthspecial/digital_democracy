## Description

The system shall let citizens declare desired outcomes (preferences) separately from specific policy arguments, recording public priorities before solutions are proposed.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Citizens can record preferences as desired outcomes
- [ ] Preferences are captured before solution proposals
- [ ] Declared preferences feed the proposal development process
