## Description

The system shall keep required participation minimal and relevant, treating citizen attention as a scarce public resource.

## Metric

- Target: Typical required civic workload stays near 2-4 hours per month; citizens see only relevant decisions.
- Measurement: Workload telemetry; relevance-of-assignments review; participation sustainability metrics.

## Traceability

- Source: White Paper Module 5 Attention-Constrained Governance; Risks 1-2 (apathy, information overload).
