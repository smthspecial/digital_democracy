## Description

The system shall remain available and resilient, avoiding single points of control or failure in operation and oversight.

## Metric

- Target: High availability target met; no single operator outage disrupts governance integrity.
- Measurement: Uptime monitoring; failover and decentralization tests; single-point-of-failure analysis.

## Traceability

- Source: White Paper Risks 16 (platform control concentration); Module 6 (no single point of authority).
