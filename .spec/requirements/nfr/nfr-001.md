## Description

The system shall preserve the secrecy of every ballot. No stored data or operator action may reveal how an identified citizen voted, and citizens must be unable to prove their vote to a third party (coercion/vote-buying resistance).

## Metric

- Target: Zero recoverable links between citizen identity and ballot content; receipt-freeness for all elections.
- Measurement: Independent cryptographic audit and red-team attempts to deanonymize ballots; coercion-resistance review.

## Traceability

- Source: White Paper Module 1 (secret ballot); Risks 7 (digital manipulation), vote buying.
