## Description

The system shall guarantee the integrity of governance records using append-only, cryptographically verifiable storage so corruption cannot be erased, only exposed.

## Metric

- Target: No undetectable modification or deletion of governance records.
- Measurement: Tamper-evidence tests; hash-chain/log integrity verification; deletion-detection drills.

## Traceability

- Source: White Paper Module 6 Immutable Audit Trail; Anti-Corruption Architecture (no hidden budgets).
