## Description

The system shall be auditable by independent parties, exposing the information needed to verify integrity without compromising privacy.

## Metric

- Target: Independent auditors can verify identity, voting, and budget integrity using public interfaces.
- Measurement: Periodic independent audits pass; audit access does not require operator cooperation.

- Source: White Paper Module 1 governance; Module 6 redundant auditing; Goal 11.
