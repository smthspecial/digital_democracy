## Description

The system shall make every significant decision, budget flow, and governance action publicly traceable by default, with opacity as the explicit exception.

## Metric

- Target: All proposals, votes, budgets, and administrative actions publicly traceable end to end.
- Measurement: Coverage audit of traceable vs. non-traceable actions; public dashboard completeness.

## Traceability

- Source: White Paper Principle 7 (Transparency is the default); Goals 3, 6, 11; Anti-Corruption Architecture.
