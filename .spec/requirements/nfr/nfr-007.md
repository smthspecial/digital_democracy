## Description

The system shall be protected as critical national infrastructure, resilient against hacking, bot attacks, coercion, vote buying, and foreign interference.

## Metric

- Target: Withstands defined threat model; no successful integrity breach in red-team exercises.
- Measurement: Penetration testing, threat-model coverage, incident metrics, foreign-interference monitoring.
