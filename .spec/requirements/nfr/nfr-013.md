## Description

The system shall be accessible across abilities and avoid digital exclusion, providing alternative access paths for citizens with limited digital access.

## Metric

- Target: Meets accessibility standards; assisted/offline-adjacent participation paths exist.
- Measurement: Accessibility (WCAG-level) audits; participation-rate analysis across demographics.

## Traceability

- Source: White Paper Social Stability section (digital exclusion); Risks 1 (apathy/participation).
