## Description

The system shall scale to national populations and large volumes of concurrent proposals, votes, and reviews without degrading correctness or responsiveness.

## Metric

- Target: Supports tens of millions of citizens and peak national-vote load within target latency.
- Measurement: Load and stress testing at projected national scale; throughput and latency under peak.

## Traceability

- Source: White Paper Goal 12; Module 4 (scalable governance); national thresholds (50,000+).
