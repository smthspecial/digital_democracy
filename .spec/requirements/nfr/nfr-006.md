## Description

The system shall protect citizens' private data. Personal address, voting history, and government identifiers shall never be publicly visible, and the identity system shall not enable surveillance.

## Metric

- Target: No public exposure of protected personal fields; no surveillance capability for operators.
- Measurement: Privacy impact assessment; data-exposure audit; access-control review.

## Traceability

- Source: White Paper Module 1 Data Model (never-public fields); Module 1 risk (government abuse).
