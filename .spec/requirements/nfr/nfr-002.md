## Description

The system shall enforce uniqueness of civic identities and prevent duplicate, fraudulent, or non-citizen participation.

## Metric

- Target: No detected duplicate identities; no successful fraudulent vote in audited elections.
- Measurement: Identity dedup audits, fraud-attempt detection rates, independent election audits.

## Traceability

- Source: White Paper Module 1 risks (identity fraud, foreign participation); Risks 7-8.
