12. Transition Strategy

One of the hardest questions:

How do we move from today’s system to this one?

You should propose gradual adoption.

Example roadmap:

Local participatory budgeting
Municipal digital voting
Advisory citizen referendums
Regional governance pilots
National hybrid systems

Realistic transition matters more than ideal theory.
